Five Pillars of Islam
اسلام کے پانچ ستون


The five pillars of Islam define the basic identity of Muslims – their faith, beliefs and practices – and bind together a worldwide community of over a billion believers into a fellowship of shared values and concerns.

Shahada (Faith) ایمان

“There is no true God but God and Muhammad is the Messenger of God.”

Namaz (Prayer) نماز

"Five daily prayers at dawn,noon,mid-afternoon,sunset and evening."

Zakat (Charity) زکات

"Fixed proportions of a Muslim’s possessions for the welfare of its neediest members."

Rozaah (Fasting) روزاہ

"Ordained in the Holy Qur’an, the fast is an act of deep personal worship in which Muslims seek a richer perception of God."

Hajj (Pilgrimage) حج

"Muslims who are physically and financially able to make the journey to Makkah, the Hajj is a once in a lifetime duty."
